Abstract

MorIARTy — Morphological Image Analysis and Recognition Toolbox.

MorIARTy stands for Morphological Image Analysis and Recognition Toolbox — a Scilab toolbox implementing the basic algorithms for Pyt'ev morphological image analysis. MorIARTy is also usable to demonstrate foundational concepts of Pyt'ev morphology such as «image shape», «projection onto image shape», and so on.
